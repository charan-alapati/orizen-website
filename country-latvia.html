Study in Latvia

Latvia offers affordable, EU-recognised degrees with English-taught programs and a growing international student community, especially in Riga and other major cities.

Why Choose Latvia?

  • EU-recognised degrees and Bologna system
  • Safe and student-friendly environment
  • Option to explore Europe on a student visa
