Study in Ireland

Ireland is a leading tech hub in Europe, hosting major companies like Google, Meta and Apple, and offering high-quality education with strong employment prospects.

  • 2-year post-study work visa (for eligible graduates)
  • Friendly, welcoming culture for international students
