Study in Germany

Germany is one of the most popular destinations for international students, offering world-class universities, low or zero tuition fees at many public institutions, and excellent career prospects in engineering, technology and research.

Why Choose Germany?

  • Low / no tuition fees at many public universities
  • Strong reputation in engineering, IT and applied sciences
  • 18-month post-study work visa after graduation
  • Central European location with great travel connectivity
  • High quality of life and strong economy

Popular Programs

  • Mechanical & Automotive Engineering
  • Mechatronics & Robotics
  • Computer Science, Data Science & AI
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Business, Management & International Economics
